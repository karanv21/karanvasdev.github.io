Hello There!

My name is Karan Vasdev and I am a 3rd-year Business and Computer Science student at the UBC Sauder School of Business in Vancouver, Canada. Moving around so often, I learned so much about people and society but the one takeaway that really stuck with me was that the world is full of problems and inefficiencies. My goal is to try and solve every problem that I can think of and make the world a better place.

When attending high school in Guangzhou, China, I ran 2 community service clubs, started green initiatives and donation programs for student-run businesses, and ran fundraisers for Student Government as Treasurer. I also started a YouTube Channel which was focused on creating Random Acts of Kindness in our school community, which you can check out here! I ran the Model UN club in my junior year, won 2 individual awards, and was a Chair at an MUN conference in South Korea with 600+ people attending. I am a member of the National Honours Society and I captained the Varsity Soccer team to it's most successful year in tournaments all over Asia, while also winning the most number of individual soccer awards in our school's history (6).

At university, I am even more motivated and ambitious. I am excelling in one of the most rigorous programs at UBC, while being on the Dean's Honour Roll and working 2 part-time jobs. I also have a range of work experiences and leadership roles in extra-curriculars that you can find below. In my free time, I participate in hackathons so I can hone my technical skills and get my creative juices flowing. I also dabble in strategy consulting while participating in some of the largest case competitions in Vancouver. I also love playing and watching soccer, meeting new people, traveling, and trying new Korean Fried Chicken restaurants in Vancouver :)
